# Privacy Policy – The Path of Today

**Service:** The Path of Today  
**Provider:** upship kim  
**Last Updated:** April 9, 2026  
**Effective Date:** April 9, 2026

---

upship kim (the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and processed when you use The Path of Today app.

---

## Article 1. Core Principle — Local-First

The Path of Today stores **all user data exclusively on your device**.

- The Company does **not collect, transmit, or store** your location, routes, emotions, photos, notes, or any other personal information on any server.
- The Company does **not operate an account system**. No sign-up is required to use the app.
- All data is permanently deleted from your device when the app is uninstalled.

---

## Article 2. Information Processed

The Company processes the following information **only on your device**.

| Item | How Collected | Purpose | Storage | Retention |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Location (GPS) | OS location services | Route tracking, map display | On-device SQLite | Until deleted by user |
| Step count | OS motion sensors | Activity tracking | On-device SQLite | Until deleted by user |
| Photos | Camera / photo library | Attaching photos to records | On-device storage | Until deleted by user |
| Mood / notes | User input | Walk journaling | On-device SQLite | Until deleted by user |
| Notification interactions | OS notification system | Reminders, activity alerts | On-device | While app is running |

> The app does **not collect** any identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

---

## Article 3. App Permissions

The app requests the following permissions. You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.

**Required**

- **Precise Location (FINE_LOCATION):** GPS coordinates for route tracking
- **Activity Recognition / Motion:** Step counting

**Optional**

- **Background Location:** Continuous route tracking when the screen is off (for long hikes or walks)
- **Notifications:** Walk start/end alerts and reminders
- **Camera / Photos:** Attaching photos to walk records

Denying optional permissions limits only the related features; all other features remain available.

---

## Article 4. Third-Party Services

The Company does not sell or share your personal information with third parties. The following tools are used to operate the Service.

| Provider | Data Processed | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Google LLC (Google Maps) | Anonymous map tile requests | Map rendering |
| Apple Inc. (Apple Maps) | Anonymous map tile requests | Map rendering (iOS) |
| Google Firebase Crashlytics | Device model, OS version, app version, stack traces | App stability and error analysis |
| Sentry (Functional Software Inc.) | Device model, OS version, app version, stack traces | App stability and error monitoring |

- These services do **not** receive your location history, mood data, or any personal content.
- Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry collect technical information only. You can disable crash reporting in app settings.

---

## Article 5. Advertising & Tracking

- The current version does **not serve behavioral advertising**.
- If advertising is introduced in the future, users will be notified in advance and iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and Android Advertising ID policies will be followed.

---

## Article 6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 14 years of age, and the Company does not knowingly collect personal information from such children.

---

## Article 7. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Access:** View all your data directly in the app
- **Correction:** Edit any record directly in the app
- **Deletion:** Delete individual records or delete the app to remove all data immediately
- **Restriction:** Revoke permissions via device settings to stop data processing

Because the Company does not store your data on servers, no formal data request process is necessary. For any inquiries, contact us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe (no later than 14 days).

---

## Article 8. International Data Transfers

The Company does not transfer your personal information outside your country. Third-party services such as Google Maps may process data on global infrastructure in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## Article 9. Security Measures

- On-device data is isolated through OS-level app sandboxing
- The database is stored in an app-private directory inaccessible to other apps
- No external network transmission occurs beyond map rendering and error reporting

---

## Article 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in response to changes in law or service policy. Material changes will be announced via in-app notice or update release notes **at least 7 days** before taking effect.
